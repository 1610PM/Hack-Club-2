THE CARTOON CHARACTER: PINK PANTHER

SOME FACTS ABOUT PINK PANTHER

This franchise began with the film "The Pink Panther", released in 1963.

The idea and creation of this character was because in the film, the pink panther was the name of a valuable pink diamond; in the credits, this was animated to be a legit pink panther.

Pink Panther is closely associated with "The Pink Panther Theme" that was composed by Henry Mancini.
